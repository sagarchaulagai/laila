 A third order digital filter has the difference equation of the form: y[n] = x[n]+2x[n-
1]+3x[n-2]-0.5 y[n-1] -0.6 y[n-2]- 0.1y[n-3] Write a MATLAB program that calculates the
poles and zeros of this digital filter.Also plot the poles and zeros of this filter.
clc; clear; close all;
b = [1 2 3];
a = [1 0.5 0.6 0.1];
% Calculate zeros and poles
zeros_f = roots(b);
poles_f = roots(a);
disp('Zeros of the digital filter:');
disp(zeros_f);
disp('Poles of the digital filter:');
disp(poles_f);
% Plot poles and zeros
figure;
zplane(b, a);
grid on;
title('Pole-Zero Plot of the Digital Filter');
